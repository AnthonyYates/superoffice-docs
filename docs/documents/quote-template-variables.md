# Quote Template - Insert template variables

The field used in step 2 is an example only. You can find all template variables in your SO\_Arc (ProductTemplateAllTemplateVariables.docx), or in the article [Creating Quote templates][1].

## How you add template variables into a quote template

1. Click **Insert** > **Quick Parts** > **Field**.

![x][img1]

2. In **Field names**, find MergeField. In **Field properties**, type your field name:

![x][img2]

3. Click **OK** and the variable will be inserted in your document:

![x][img3]
