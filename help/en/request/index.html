Requests

Requests is the main feature of SuperOffice Service. This is where you manage requests and enquiries that are in the system. You can also create internal and external requests, respond to incoming enquiries and obtain an overview of the statuses of all requests in the system. When you open SuperOffice Service or click Dashboard in the Navigator, you arrive at the Dashboard, which provides an overview of request handling. Click Requests to show relevant options for requests.
