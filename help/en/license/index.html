Licences

In the SuperOffice tab in the Licences screen, you can display and update the licence information for your version of SuperOffice CRM and associated modules.

Note

If you have installed a third-party module or similar, several tabs will be displayed here.

At the top is the company name (your company/organisation) and serial number.

If the licence is time-limited, the expiry date is displayed below the serial number.

Activate licences

Above the licence list is the update status of your licences. You can click the link to display information on the update and make any changes.

The following statuses can be shown here:

  • New licence activated – the system has updated the licence automatically. You can click the link to view the changes.

  • Activate new licence – new licence is available, but it cannot be activated automatically. Click the link to open the Activate new licence dialog. The list will only show altered licences.

    Is the Activate button disabled? Is the licence name shown in red? This may be because the total number of new licences is less than the number of licences in use. You will need to reduce the number of user licences in use. See Edit licence information.

Note

If you are using a demo version of SuperOffice CRM, you may be asked to enter a new serial number and licence owner. You will be given this by SuperOffice when you purchase a SuperOffice licence.

Licences

The list at the bottom shows which licences are in use and how many are available. Click a licence name to display information about a licence.

Purchase licences

Click the Purchase new licences button to go to SuperOffice's online store SuperMarket, where you can purchase new licences.
