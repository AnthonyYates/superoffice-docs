Companies

The Company screen contains all your company's customers. For each company, you can view the associated contacts in the Contacts section tab.

Note

Contacts that are not associated with a company, can be found in the Contact screen.

The bottom of the screen contains the following default section tabs:

  • Contacts
  • Relations
  • Projects
  • Activities
  • Sales
  • Requests

Everything you need to find out about a company is available from a single screen.
