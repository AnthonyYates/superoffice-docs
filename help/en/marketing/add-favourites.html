Add favourite

The Favourites function gives you easy to access to your favourite mailings or forms.

Add a mailing as a favourite

To add a mailing or form to your favourites, open it and click the star icon in the top-right corner. The star turns yellow , and the create-mailing/form is instantly added to your favourites.

In SuperOffice Service you can now quickly access the record by clicking Marketing and then in the Navigator.

Tip

If you hold the mouse pointer over a mailing or form in the favourites list, a tooltip is displayed containing additional data about the mailing or form.
