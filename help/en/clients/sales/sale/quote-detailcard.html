Quote (section tab in Sale)

In the Quote section tab at the bottom of the Sale screen, you can display existing quotes or create new ones. The options available to you here depend on whether a quote has been created for the sale, or not.

Sale without an existing quote

Create a new quote: Click here to create a quote for this sale. See Create a quote.

Copy quote from another sale: Click here to use an existing quote at the basis for a new quote. See Copy a quote.

Sale with an existing quote

Click to create quote document: See Create a quote document.

Alternatives: If the quote contains one or more alternatives, you can choose one from the list at the top of the Quote section tab. The / icons at the bottom of the list indicate whether the selected alternative is the favourite alternative or not.

On the right of the Quote section tab are the version, quote number and status (for example, Draft or Sold). Click Open to edit the quote. See the Edit quote dialog.

The product list: If products have been added to the quote, they are shown in the list. At the bottom of the list, the total price of the products in it is displayed. See Add/edit a product in the quote.

Export: Click here to export the product list to a spreadsheet.
