General - Currency

In the Sale screen tabs in SuperOffice CRM, you specify sale amount, costs and profit for the sale. The currency that the amounts are shown in depends on what you specify in the Preferences screen in Settings and maintenance (see Preferences), where you choose your own currency and a base currency, from a list that is defined in the Lists screen.

Note

To enable use of currency, go to Preferences > Enable use of currency.
