Delete email filters

To delete an email address from SuperOffice Service, do as follows:

  1. Go to the E-mail tab:

    • From Settings and Maintenance: Click the Requests button in the navigator and select the E-mail tab.

    • From Service: Select System settings > E-mail.

  1. Select the E-mail filters tab.

  2. Click the required email filter. The Properties for e-mail filter screen appears.

  3. Click the Delete button at the bottom of the screen.

  4. Click OK when you are asked if you want to delete the email filter. The email filter is removed from SuperOffice Service.
