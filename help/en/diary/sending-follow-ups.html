Send follow-ups

To send a follow-up as an e-mail:

  1. Right-click the required follow-up on the Activities section tab in the Company, Contact, Diary, Sale or Project screens, and select Send as e-mail.

  2. A message is opened in your e-mail program:

    By default, the address of a specified contact is entered in the To field.

    • The follow-up type and description from the dialog for follow-ups is inserted in the subject field.
    • Edit the e-mail message as required. For example, you can add more recipients. Note, however, that this information will not be updated in SuperOffice CRM.

  3. Click Send to send the message.
