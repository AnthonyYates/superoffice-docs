Add follow-ups (appointments, tasks and phone calls)

To create follow-ups in SuperOffice CRM, you can click the New button on the top bar.

Tip

You can also add follow-ups by double-clicking an empty line in the Activities section tab. By default this opens the Appointment dialog. You can specify which dialog should open under Preferences ( Personal settings > Preferences > Functions > Type from Activities section tab).
