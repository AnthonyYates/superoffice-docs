Global buttons

The global buttons appear throughout the user interface. Some of them are in the main screen, while others are in the various dialogs.

Global buttons in Standard CRM

Button Function
Click here to open the search window.
Click here to choose a telephone number to dial.
Click an arrow button to open and close a list box.
Click here to open a menu containing tasks relevant to the dialog or screen.

Global buttons in Service

Button Description
Click here to add information, such as e-mail addresses or new contacts or companies.
Click here to open a menu containing tasks relevant to the dialog or screen.
Click here to open and close a list box.
Click here to add the current record (request, contact or similar) as a favorite.
Click here to start a search.
Click here to configure the appearance and behavior of a screen element (such as a list of search results, a properties view or a request header). See Profile.
Click here to select a date from the calendar.
