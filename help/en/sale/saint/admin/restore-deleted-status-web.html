Restore a deleted status

Note

Status monitors require the use of Sales Premium plan or, if you are using an earlier version of SuperOffice installed on your premises, a separate license (Sales Intelligence).

To restore a deleted status:

  1. Open the SAINT screen.
  2. Select the Company, Project or Contact tab.
  1. Check Show deleted below the status list. Deleted statuses are clearly marked.
  2. Double-click the status you want to restore. The Status monitor dialog opens.
  3. Click Restore and then Save. The status is restored and appears in black in the list.
  4. Click Regenerate selected status monitor to update the restored status.
