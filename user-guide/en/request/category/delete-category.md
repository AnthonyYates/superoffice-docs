# Delete categories

To delete a category:

[!include[Go to category](includes/goto-category.md)]

1. Click the category name. This category opens.

    > [!TIP]
    > If the category is a subcategory, you first need to click the arrow to the left of the parent category in order to display the associated subcategories.

1. Click the **Delete** button at the bottom of the screen. The **Delete request category** dialog appears.

1. Click **OK**.

1. In the **Transfer requests to** list, specify where to move the requests in this category.

1. Click **OK**. The category is deleted and all the requests are moved to the specified alternative category.
