# Open and view a request

There are multiple ways to access a request.

* [Pre-defined lists][1]
* [Search][2]
* [Favorites][4]
* [Flagged messages][5]

When you double-click a row in the [search results screen][3], you are taken to the **Request** screen.

## What do you want to do now?

* [Accept the request][6]
* [Reply][7]
* [Transfer][8]
* [Close][9]
