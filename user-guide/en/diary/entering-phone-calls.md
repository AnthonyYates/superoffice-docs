# Enter phone calls

To enter a new call:

* Choose **New** &gt; **Phone Call** on the [top bar][2].
* Click **Task** ( ![icon][img1] ) &gt; **Make a phone call** in a contact card.

The **Phone call** dialog opens. Here you specify the phone call details as described under [The Dialog for follow-ups][1].
