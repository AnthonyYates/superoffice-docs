# Delete activities in the activities list

To delete activities in the activities list, select one or more activities and click **Delete** at the bottom left of the list. A dialog opens asking you to confirm or cancel the deletion. [!include[Delete elements](includes/see-delete-elements.md)]

> [!NOTE]
> The **Delete** button is only available if the user who is logged in has permission to delete items from the activities list in question.
