# Delete companies

To delete a company and associated data:

1. [!include[Go to company](../includes/goto-company.md)]
2. Click the ![icon][img1] **Task** button and select **Delete &lt;company name&gt;**.
3. The **Delete Company** dialog opens. Click **Yes** to confirm that you really want to delete the company. (For more details about this, see [Delete items in SuperOffice CRM][1].)
