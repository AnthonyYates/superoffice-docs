# MAILING_TempVar_example

<p>Instead of this text:</p>
<p>&quot;Dear Customer. Would you like to know more about how our product 
 can help your company win new customers? Please call us at SuperShop.&quot;</p>
<p>you can send this one:</p>
<p>&quot;Dear Chris. Would you like to know more about how our product 
 can help Car Care Ltd win new customers? Please call Ryan Webster at SuperShop.&quot;</p>
<p>The text you enter will look like this:</p>
<p>&quot;Dear <span class="UI-text">[[customer.firstname]]</span>. Would 
 you like to know more about how our product can help <span class="UI-text">[[company.name]]</span> 
 win new customers? Please call <span class="UI-text">[[company.ourSalesContact.name]]</span> 
 at SuperShop.&quot;</p>
