<p>In the final step, <span class="UI-text">Confirm</span>, you can view 
 a summary of the mailing and verify that these are correct. Any error 
 messages displayed in this screen need to be solved before the mailing 
 can be sent.</p>
<p class="Note"><span style="font-weight: bold;">Note</span>: The send 
 options are disabled if there are incomplete steps or missing information 
 in your mailing. These are indicated in red.</p>
<p class="Prosedyre">Send the mailing now <img src="Mailing/Bitmaps/Loc/btn_sendnow.png" 
 alt="" style="border: none;" 
 border="0" /></p>
<p>Click <span class="UI-text">Send now</span> to send the mailing immediately. 
 The workflow closes and you are returned to the <span class="UI-text">Mailings</span> 
 tab.</p>
<p>Click the mailing in the list to view sending status and statistics. 
 See <a href="Mailing/Helptopics/Track_the_mailing_and_view_statistics.html">Track 
 the mailing and view statistics</a>.</p>
<p class="Prosedyre">Schedule to send <img src="Mailing/Bitmaps/Loc/btn_schedule.png" 
 alt="" style="border: none;" 
 border="0" /></p>
<p>In order not to overload your servers during working hours, it might 
 be a good idea to choose a time during the evening. This option is not 
 available for mailings that have already started, temporarily stopped 
 or have finished.</p>
<p>To do this:</p>
<ol style="list-style: decimal;">
<li>Click <span class="UI-text">Schedule</span>.</li>
<li>Under <span class="UI-text">Schedule to send at</span>, select 
 one of the following options: 
<ul>
<li>Click the <span class="UI-text">Choose date</span> icon ( <img 
 src="Mailing/Bitmaps/btn_calendar.jpg" alt="Icon_select time period.bmp" 
 width="14" height="14" border="0" /> ), and select the date and 
 time the mailing is to start.</li>
<li>Click <b>Set to now</b> ( <img src="Mailing/Bitmaps/btn_schedulenow.png" 
 alt="" style="border: none;" 
 border="0" /> ) to insert 
 today's date and the time.</li>
</ul></li>
<li>Click <span class="UI-text">OK</span>. The workflow closes and 
 you are returned to the <span class="UI-text">Mailings</span> tab.</li>
</ol>
<p class="Prosedyre">What happens now?</p>
<p>You can click the mailing in the list to view sending status and statistics. 
 See <a href="Mailing/Helptopics/Track_the_mailing_and_view_statistics.html">Track 
 the mailing and view statistics</a>.</p>
<p>To stop the mailing or start a planned mailing ahead of schedule, click 
 the <span class="UI-text">Stop</span> or <span class="UI-text">Start</span> 
 button respectively.</p>
