# quote_select_ERP

<p>If the <span style="font-weight: bold;">Select ERP connection</span> 
 dialog is displayed when you are creating a quote, this may be due to:</p>
<ul style="list-style: disc;">
<li>No price lists being available for the currency of the sale. You 
 need to select another currency for the sale, or create a price list 
 with the desired currency in SuperOffice Settings and maintenance.</li>
</ul>
<p class="Note"><span style="font-weight: bold;">Note:</span> If the list 
 of currencies is not displayed next to the sale amount, you may have to 
 enable use of currency. You can do this from <span style="font-weight: bold;">Preferences</span> 
 &gt; <span style="font-weight: bold;">Enable use of currency</span> in 
 SuperOffice Settings and maintenance.</p>
<ul style="list-style: disc;">
<li><?rh-udv_start name="SO_User" ?>SuperOffice CRM<?rh-udv_end ?> 
 is connected to one or more ERP systems, and there are several relevant 
 ERP connections to choose between. The ERP connection determines where 
 the product information and prices are obtained from.</li>
</ul>
