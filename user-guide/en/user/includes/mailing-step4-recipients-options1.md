<p class="Prosedyre">Recipient options</p>
<p>Under <span class="UI-text">Options</span> in the lower part of the 
 <span class="UI-text">Recipients</span> screen, you can select various 
 alternatives for the mailing:</p>
<ul>
<li><span class="UI-text">Override consent and subscription</span>: 
 Select this option to also send the mailing to contacts who have not 
 given their consent to receiving e-marketing or are not subscribing 
 to this mailing type. These contacts are listed with the status <span 
 class="UI-text">No consent</span> in the <b>Recipients</b> list.</li>
</ul>
