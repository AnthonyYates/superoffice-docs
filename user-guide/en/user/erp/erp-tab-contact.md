# The ERP tab (Contact screen)

If your organisation has a licence for SuperOffice Integration Server, the **ERP** tab is displayed in the **Contact** screen. If a company is connected to the ERP system, any contacts associated with this company can also be connected to a matching individual in the ERP system.
