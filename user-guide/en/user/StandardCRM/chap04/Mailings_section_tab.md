# Mailings (tab in Selection)

Here you can create a mailing to contacts in a selection. You can also see the mailings that have been archived on this selection.

The purpose of the latter is to keep track of all mailings related to a selection.

Double-click a mailing to open it.

**Create a mailing**: Click here to create a new mailing to the contacts in this selection. See [Step 1: Setup](../../Mailing/Helptopics/step_1_setup_email.md).

**Export**: Click here to export the list to a spreadsheet.
