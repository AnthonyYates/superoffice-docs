# View email messages

When you select a message in the inbox, a preview of the message is displayed in right part of the screen.

The **Preview** tab contains the following fields:

\[Subject\]

Shows the subject of the email.

\[From\]

Shows the sender. If the sender is recorded in SuperOffice CRM, you can hold the mouse-pointer over the name to view the contact card. See also [Add email addresses in SuperOffice CRM][1].

**Date**

Shows the date and time of the message.

To

Shows the recipient(s) of the message.

Attachments

Displays any attachments to the message. See [Archive or open attachments][2] for more information about your options here.

> [!NOTE]
> Go to ![icon][img1] **Personal settings &gt; email options**. Open the **General** tab. If you check **Show inline images**, inline images will be displayed in preview.
