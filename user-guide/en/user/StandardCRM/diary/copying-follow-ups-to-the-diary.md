# Copy follow-ups to the Diary

Copying a follow-up to another time or day in the diary uses exactly the same procedure as moving a follow-up. (See [Move follow-ups to a different time](Moving-follow-ups-to-a-different-time.md)). However, when copying, you need to hold down the **CTRL** key while you drag and drop.
