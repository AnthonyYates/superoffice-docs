# Open follow-ups

Open an existing follow-up by double-clicking it:

* On the **Activities** section tab for the active company in the **Company** screen.
* On the **Activities** section tab for the active company in the **Contact** screen.
* On the **Activities** section tab for the active sale in the **Sale** screen.
* On the **Activities** section tab for the active project in the **Project** screen.
* On the **Activities** section tab in the **Diary** screen.
* On the different tabs in the **Diary** screen.

This brings up the **Appointment**, **Task** or **Phone Call** dialog, depending on the type of follow-up.
