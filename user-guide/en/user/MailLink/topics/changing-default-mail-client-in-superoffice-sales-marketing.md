# Changing default mail client in SuperOffice

If you want to change mail client, it is possible to change clients in the **Preferences** screen in SuperOffice CRM.

Follow these steps:

1. Open SuperOffice CRM.
2. Choose ![icon](../../../../media/icons/globalmenu-personalsettings-small.png)**Personal settings &gt; Preferences**. The **Preferences** dialog opens.
3. In the navigator on the left-hand side of the **Preferences** dialog, select **E-mail**.
4. In the **E-mail client** field, select your preferred mail client.
5. Click **Save**.
6. Next time you click **Inbox** in the navigator of SuperOffice CRM, the mail client you selected will open.

> [!TIP]
> In SuperOffice CRM for Web you may also change default mail client in the login screen. When you click **Settings**, an **E-mail client** field will appear.
