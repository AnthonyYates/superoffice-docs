# Buttons in the e-mail window

These buttons appear in the new e-mail window in your mail client (see [Creating e-mails from Microsoft Outlook](Creating-e-mails-from-Microsoft-Outlook.md) or [Creating e-mails from Lotus Notes](Creating-e-mails-from-Lotus-Notes.md)):

<table data-cellspacing="0">
<tbody>
<tr>
<td><p><strong>Button</strong></p></td>
<td><p><strong>Function</strong></p></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><p>![icon](../media/Archive-is-on.png)</p>
<p>![icon](../media/Archive-is-off.png)</p></td>
<td><p><strong>Archive is ON / Archive is OFF</strong>: Indicates whether or not the e-mail will be archived when sent. Click the button to enable or disable this setting. The default setting is specified in the **Archive options** pane of the <strong>SuperOffice CRM options</strong> dialog. See [Archive options](Default-options.md).</p></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><p>![icon](../media/Add-recipient.png)</p></td>
<td><p>**Add recipient**: Click here to open the **Select recipients** dialog. See [Selecting recipients](Selecting-recipients.md).</p></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><p>![icon](../media/Add-document.png)</p></td>
<td><p><strong>**Attach document**</strong>: Click here to open the **Include attachments** dialog. See [Adding documents from SuperOffice CRM as attachments](Adding-documents-from-SuperOffice-Sales-Marketing-as-attachments.md).</p></td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td><p>![icon](../media/SuperOffice-button.png)</p></td>
<td><p>Click the small arrow to the right of the **SuperOffice** button to open the <strong>SuperOffice CRM options</strong> dialog. See [SuperOffice CRM options dialog](E-mail-options-dialog.md).</p></td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>
