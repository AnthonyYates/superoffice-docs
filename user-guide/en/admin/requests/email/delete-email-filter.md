# Delete email filters

To delete an email address from SuperOffice Service, do as follows:

1. Click the **Requests** button in the navigator and select the **E-mail** tab.

2. Select the **E-mail filters** tab.

3. Click the required email filter. The **Properties for e-mail filter** screen appears.

4. Click the **Delete** button at the bottom of the screen.

5. Click **OK** when you are asked if you want to delete the email filter. The email filter is removed from SuperOffice Service.
